About Quaerens

Clear support when complaints get complicated.

Quaerens was created to help people who feel overwhelmed dealing with companies that ignore complaints, delay refunds, or reject complaints or claims in a way that feels unfair.

We specialise in organising your evidence and managing the practical side of consumer disputes — from initial review to clear next steps.

Why We Exist

We understand how stressful and confusing disputes can be. Many people have a strong case but struggle because their documents are scattered and their communications are not structured. We help bring order and clarity so you can present your case more effectively.

Founder Story

Quaerens was founded by a small team of professionals with years of combined experience in consumer rights, complaints handling, and dispute resolution. After repeatedly seeing people with strong cases lose out simply because their evidence was disorganised and their complaints poorly structured, the team decided to create a calmer, more practical service — one that puts clear evidence and realistic support at the centre.

Organised evidence

Quaerens helps people organise consumer dispute evidence, understand practical complaint routes and prepare clearer next steps.

Clearer review

Many disputes become difficult because documents are scattered across emails, screenshots, statements, contracts, support tickets and complaint replies.

How The Process Works

1Initial information

You share a short summary of your situation, the amount involved (if relevant), and any documents or replies you already have.

2Evidence review

We carefully review everything to identify strengths, gaps, and what else would help strengthen your position.

3Practical next steps

We prepare and organise clearer complaints or escalations. This may include using one of our free DIY tools or, for more complex cases, providing guided support with document preparation and filing.

Fees And Paid Support

Many people successfully resolve their issues using our free tools and guides.

Paid support is available only when we believe it is genuinely beneficial. Scope and fees are always discussed clearly upfront.

Legal And Regulatory Position

Where a matter appears to need legal, regulated financial or specialist professional advice, this should be made clear.

Our Approach

  • Evidence-first
  • Transparent and realistic
  • Free tools and guidance available first
  • No pressure or unrealistic promises

What Quaerens Does

Quaerens can help with:

  • Organising documents and timelines
  • Identifying useful evidence
  • Preparing clearer complaint summaries
  • Helping visitors understand possible escalation routes
  • Supporting practical next steps where a dispute has become difficult to manage

Quaerens does not guarantee compensation. The strength of any complaint depends on the facts, documents, timing, responses received and the route available.
