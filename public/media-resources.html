Plain-English consumer dispute resources, free tools and evidence checklists for journalists, bloggers, community groups and consumer information pages.

About Quaerens

Quaerens is a UK consumer dispute support platform that helps people organise evidence, understand practical complaint routes and prepare next steps. Quaerens is not a law firm and does not guarantee outcomes.

Linking and attribution

These resources may be referenced from articles, local advice pages, community websites, blogs and useful-links pages where they help readers understand a practical consumer complaint route. Please describe Quaerens as a consumer dispute support and evidence organisation service.
