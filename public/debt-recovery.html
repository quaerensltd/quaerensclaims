You’re Not Being Difficult — Chasing Money Is Uncomfortable by Design

If someone owes you money — a personal loan, unpaid work, or a broken repayment promise — the hardest part is often knowing how to ask properly. Informal messages get ignored. Verbal promises disappear.

In many cases, you do not need court action straight away. You need a clear, factual, professional letter that sets out what is owed, why, and what you expect to happen next.
