Solar Panel Finance Not Properly Explained?

If your solar system was sold with credit, a loan or monthly payments that were unclear or unaffordable, Quaerens can help organise the evidence for review.

✅ Monthly payments were not explained clearly ✅ The finance cost outweighed expected savings ✅ You felt pressured to sign quickly ✅ Important terms, risks or cancellation rights were unclear
Start Free Review Back to Main Guide

Why this matters

Many solar disputes are not only about the panels. The way the finance was presented, the affordability checks, and the link between promised savings and monthly payments may all be important.

What to gather

Finance agreements, direct debit records, sales notes, savings estimates, lender correspondence and installer material can help build the picture.
