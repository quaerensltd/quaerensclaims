What are your rights?

UK consumer law protects you against unfair, misleading, or aggressive business practices. Under the:

  • Consumer Protection from Unfair Trading Regulations 2008 – Prohibits businesses from using misleading information or aggressive sales tactics. If a trader deceives or pressures you into a contract, you may have the right to claim damages or cancel the agreement.
  • Consumer Rights Act 2015 – Gives consumers clear rights when buying goods, services, or digital content. You are entitled to fair treatment, clear terms, and remedies such as refunds or repairs if the service is substandard or not as described.
  • Misrepresentation Act 1967 – Protects consumers who entered into contracts based on false or misleading statements. If a misrepresentation influenced your decision, you may be entitled to cancel the contract or claim compensation.

…you may have legal grounds to cancel your contract, seek compensation, or both.
