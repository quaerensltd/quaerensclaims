Platform failure evidence

What evidence strengthens a digital platform dispute?

Google searchers often arrive here after a stressful event: a blocked withdrawal, frozen app account, missing balance, failed booking, rejected order, trade execution error or repeated support message that does not answer the real issue. The review needs to show the exact event, value affected and response history.

Screenshots, logs and timestamps

Capture app screens, account notices, error codes, status pages, email headers and chat transcripts showing the date and sequence of events.

Money trail and account records

Use bank statements, card records, wallet addresses, invoices, order references, refund promises and balance histories to identify the amount affected.

Complaint replies and decision reasons

Keep every support ticket, automated reply, final response and reason given for refusing access, refund, withdrawal or correction.

Quaerens can prepare a free, no-obligation assessment report explaining what evidence appears strong, what remains unclear and which escalation route may fit the documents.

Find the right route

Different platform problems need different evidence routes

Blocked or frozen account

Focus on access notices, identity checks, balance records, payment restrictions and complaint replies explaining why funds were held.

Crypto or investment-style platform

Separate genuine platform failure from possible scam indicators such as fake dashboards, pressure messages, blocked withdrawals or new fee demands.

Card, finance or refund angle

Where a card payment or regulated account is involved, the evidence may need to support a chargeback, Section 75 or formal complaint route.
