Common problem

Was the mortgage advice truly suitable for your circumstances?

Many borrowers only realise later that a mortgage, remortgage, or secured loan came with fees, risks, repayment pressures, or long-term consequences that were not properly explained.

A review can help check whether the recommendation, affordability assessment, broker conduct, fees, or borrowing structure appear fair and suitable.

You may have grounds for a review if:

  • • You paid high broker or arrangement fees
  • • The repayments became difficult soon after completion
  • • You consolidated debts but ended up worse off
  • • A secured or second charge loan was recommended
  • • Cheaper or safer alternatives were not discussed
  • • You did not fully understand the risks before agreeing
