Who may want this review?

You may want to check your mortgage if:

  • You paid high broker or arrangement fees
    Especially where cheaper or more suitable options were not properly explained.
  • The borrowing became hard to manage
    Repayments becoming unaffordable soon after completion can be a warning sign.
  • You were placed into the wrong type of product
    This can include remortgages, secured loans, or debt consolidation arrangements.
  • You still feel the deal was never right for your circumstances
    A structured review can help clarify whether that concern deserves deeper assessment.
