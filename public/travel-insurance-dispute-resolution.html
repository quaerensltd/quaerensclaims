Useful evidence

What documents can help with a travel insurance dispute review?

  • Policy wording and insurance schedule
    These help identify the cover, exclusions, limits and conditions the insurer is relying on.
  • Claim decision and insurer correspondence
    Refusal letters, calculation notes and emails help show why the claim was rejected or reduced.
  • Receipts, medical records and travel documents
    Supporting documents help prove the loss, reason for claim and amount affected.
  • Complaint history and evidence sent
    A clear record of what was submitted and when can help identify gaps or unfair handling.

Issues commonly reviewed:

  • Rejected medical, cancellation or baggage claim
  • Pre-existing condition or disclosure dispute
  • Underpaid or reduced settlement offer
  • Delayed claim decision or repeated document requests
  • Policy exclusion or wording dispute
