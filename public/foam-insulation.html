Your rights

What should have happened before and during installation?

Homeowners should receive accurate information about risks, proper installation, and the likely long-term impact of spray foam insulation on the property.

  • Clear information before installation – Risks such as mortgage issues, ventilation concerns, and removal difficulties should be explained properly.
  • Proper workmanship – Insulation should be installed with reasonable care and in a way that does not create avoidable problems.
  • Possible remedies if issues arise – Where there has been poor advice, misrepresentation, or poor installation, it may be possible to seek recovery or resolution.
