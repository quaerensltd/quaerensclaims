Your Rights for Lost, Delayed or Damaged Luggage

  • Lost Bags: Up to €1,600/£1,350 compensation under the Montreal Convention (1999).
  • Delayed Bags: Claim for essentials purchased while waiting (toiletries, clothes, etc).
  • Damaged Bags: Airlines must repair or compensate for damage.
  • All major airlines are covered (EU, UK, international).
  • Letter works for Ryanair, easyJet, BA, Lufthansa, Wizz Air, KLM, Vueling, Jet2, and more.

What Evidence Helps?

  • Boarding pass or booking confirmation
  • Baggage tag (the sticker given at check-in)
  • Property Irregularity Report (PIR) from airport
  • Receipts for essentials you had to buy
  • Photos (for damaged items)
  • Email or chat evidence from the airline (if any)

You don’t need everything—just upload what you have. We'll explain how to use it in your claim.
