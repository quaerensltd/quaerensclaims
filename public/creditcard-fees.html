Credit card fee evidence

Reasons credit card fees or add-ons may deserve review

Credit card disputes can involve unclear charges, repeated fees, packaged add-ons, interest calculations, disputed transactions or complaint replies that do not explain how the balance was calculated.

Fees were repeated or poorly explained

Statements, notices, arrears letters and transaction histories can show whether charges built up fairly or were applied without clear explanation.

An add-on or payment protection product was unclear

Application records, call notes and policy documents can help identify whether an optional product was properly explained.

The lender has not broken down the figures

Where a provider gives a generic reply, a clearer schedule of charges, dates and complaint points may strengthen the escalation.
