Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 May 2025

Quaerens Ltd is committed to protecting your personal data and your privacy. We act strictly as a consulting agent and work with external legal professionals to provide you with services related to consumer and financial claims. Quaerens Ltd is not a law firm and does not offer legal advice directly.

1. What We Collect

  • Your name, email address, and contact details
  • Details about your claim (e.g., documents, summaries, dates)
  • Technical data such as browser type, IP address, and usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To evaluate whether you may have a valid claim
  • To communicate with you and provide status updates
  • To refer your case to qualified legal professionals where appropriate

3. Lawful Basis

We process data under GDPR lawful bases including contract, consent, and legitimate interest.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share data with our trusted legal partners to pursue your claim, and with service providers (like email or hosting) where needed.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy and as required by applicable law.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request rectification or deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

7. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, email us at info@quaerens.co.uk.

This policy is reviewed and updated as needed. Any changes will be published on this page.
