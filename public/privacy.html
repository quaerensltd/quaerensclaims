Who we are

Quaerens Ltd is a UK-based consumer consultancy that helps individuals prepare documents, organise evidence and understand next steps for consumer, financial and dispute-related matters.

Information we may collect

  • Full name and contact details such as email address and phone number.
  • Country of residence and preferred language.
  • Request-specific information such as the type of issue, financial details, documents and supporting evidence you choose to send.
  • IP address, browser type and usage data through cookies and analytics tools.

How we use your information

  • Review and assess your request.
  • Communicate with you about your case or enquiry.
  • Prepare documents, summaries or next-step guidance.
  • Connect you with trusted external legal partners where specialist advice is needed.
  • Maintain records, comply with legal obligations and improve our website and services.

Sharing your information

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share information with external lawyers or law firms where this is relevant to your request and with your knowledge or consent.
  • We may use IT, hosting, analytics and operational service providers under appropriate data protection arrangements.
  • We may share information with authorities where required by law.

Your rights

  • You may request access to the data we hold about you.
  • You may ask us to correct inaccurate or outdated information.
  • You may request deletion where legally permissible.
  • You may object to certain uses of your data or withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

Security and retention

  • We use technical and organisational measures such as secure systems, restricted access and backups to protect information.
  • We retain data only for as long as needed for the purpose it was provided, legal requirements, dispute handling and legitimate business records.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to understand site usage, improve functionality and remember preferences. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.
