Your rights

What should you expect from a solar provider?

Homeowners and businesses should receive a solar system that is installed with reasonable care and skill, works safely, and performs as described.

  • A properly functioning system – Panels, inverters, batteries, and related components should operate safely and as expected.
  • Reasonable repairs and support – Providers should respond appropriately to faults, service issues, and warranty concerns.
  • Clear remedies where things go wrong – Where systems are defective, unsafe, or misrepresented, there may be routes to repair, replacement, or financial redress.
