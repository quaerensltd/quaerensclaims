Claim Compensation for a Delayed or Failed Switch

If your energy supplier switch took too long, failed, or left you out of contract, you have a right to automatic compensation under Ofgem’s Guaranteed Standards. This includes switches between electricity and gas suppliers in England, Scotland, and Wales.

  • Switch Delayed: If your switch took longer than 5 working days, you may be owed £30 per fuel.
  • Erroneous Switch: If you were switched without your permission, you can claim for each issue (£30–£60).
  • No Final Bill/Refund: Suppliers must provide a final bill within 6 weeks. Missed? More compensation due.
  • Compensation is direct: Paid by your supplier—no legal fees or agents needed.

What Are My Rights?

  • Ofgem Guaranteed Standards: £30 per fuel for delays or mistakes when switching suppliers (from 5 working days after signup).
  • Final Bill Standard: You must get your final bill within 6 weeks—if not, compensation is due.
  • Direct Payment: The supplier pays you directly—no middleman or agent can take a cut.
  • Energy Ombudsman: If your supplier does not pay or respond, escalate your claim for free.
