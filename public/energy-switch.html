Situation

Energy switches should be handled quickly and correctly

If your energy supplier switch was delayed, failed, completed without your permission, or caused billing problems, you may have a right to compensation under Ofgem’s Guaranteed Standards.

These rules can apply to gas and electricity switches in England, Scotland, and Wales. Compensation is paid directly by the supplier, and you do not need to use a claims company.

Common issues worth checking:

  • • Switch took longer than expected
  • • Switched without your permission
  • • Final bill was delayed
  • • Refund was not sent on time
  • • Supplier failed to correct the problem

What can you claim for?

Typical energy switch compensation issues

  • Switch delayed
    If your switch took too long, compensation may be due per fuel.
  • Erroneous switch
    If you were switched without permission, your supplier should correct the issue and may owe compensation.
  • Final bill delay
    Suppliers must issue final bills within required timescales.
  • Refund delay
    Credit balances and refunds should be handled promptly once the account is closed.
