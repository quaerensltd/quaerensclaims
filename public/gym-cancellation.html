Your rights

What rights may apply?

Consumer Rights Act 2015

In the UK, contract terms must be fair and transparent. Unreasonable cancellation terms or hidden conditions may be challengeable.

EU consumer protections

EU consumer rules can provide extra protection, especially where a contract was entered online, by phone, or without clear information.

Cooling-off rights

Depending on how and where you joined, a 14-day cooling-off period may apply to some gym contracts.

Changed circumstances

Relocation, health issues, financial hardship, closures, or major service changes may support a reasonable cancellation request.
