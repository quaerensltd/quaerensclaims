What Can You Claim Holiday Compensation For?

    • ✅ Cancelled or Severely Delayed Package Holidays: Full refund and compensation based on Directive (EU) 2015/2302.
    • ✅ Holiday Not as Described (Misrepresentation): Refund for issues like closed pools, building works, or inaccurate hotel descriptions.
    • ✅ Illness or Injury During a Package Holiday: Claims for poor hygiene, unsafe conditions, or food poisoning.
    • ✅ Flight Delays, Cancellations or Overbooking: Compensation under EU Regulation EC 261/2004.
    • ✅ Travel Company Insolvency: Claims via schemes like ATOL (UK) or SGR (Netherlands).

Legal Protections for Holidaymakers

    • EU Package Travel Directive (EU 2015/2302)
    • UK Package Travel Regulations (2018)
    • EU Flight Compensation Regulation (EC 261/2004)
    • Consumer Rights Directive (2011/83/EU)
