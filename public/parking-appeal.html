Challenge Your Parking Fine

If you’ve received a parking ticket or Penalty Charge Notice (PCN), you may be able to overturn it with a well-written appeal. Many fines are cancelled every year because of unclear signs, genuine mistakes, technical errors, or mitigating circumstances.

  • Unclear or missing signs—photos can win your appeal.
  • Medical or emergency reasons—often accepted if explained.
  • Payment machine not working—keep any evidence.
  • Incorrect details or errors—wrong car reg, date, location.
  • Mitigating circumstances—honest mistakes, childcare, disability, etc.

What Are My Rights?

  • Parking fines must be fair and clearly communicated—signage must be clear and accurate.
  • You have the right to challenge any fine—before payment, you can submit an appeal explaining your situation.
  • Appeals can succeed for genuine mistakes, technical errors, or mitigating circumstances.
  • Don't pay under protest—wait for your appeal decision before paying, unless instructed otherwise.
