Denied boarding evidence

What to include when you were denied boarding

Denied boarding claims often depend on whether you checked in on time, had valid travel documents, and whether the airline refused boarding because of overbooking or another operational issue.

Proof you were ready to travel

Boarding passes, check-in confirmations, passport/visa evidence and airport arrival timing can support the letter.

Airline explanation at the gate

Write down what staff said, whether volunteers were requested and whether overbooking was mentioned.

Replacement journey and expenses

Keep details of the alternative flight, delay on arrival, food, transport and accommodation costs.
