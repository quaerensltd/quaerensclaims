Timeshare review

What this guide helps you understand

Why duration matters

Timeshare contracts that continue for decades, or appear to continue indefinitely, can be a major reason owners seek a review. The duration, renewal wording and inheritance wording should be checked carefully.

Evidence that helps

Useful evidence includes the contract, membership rules, certificate documents, fee demands, resort replies and anything said during the sales process about resale, inheritance or future exit.

What Quaerens provides
