The problem

Many homeowners were not told the full solar story.

Many solar problems start with promises about savings, performance, finance or warranties that later do not match the documents or the system output.

The key issue is often proving what was said, what was signed, how the system performed and how the installer, lender, warranty provider or complaint route responded.

You may need the paperwork and timeline organised before anyone can give the issue a fair review.

Do you qualify?

You may have a review route if any of this has happened.

  • ✓Your panels are generating less than you were led to expect
  • ✓The finance, loan or payment terms were not clearly explained
  • ✓Your installer is no longer responding or has gone bust
  • ✓You have missing MCS, warranty, handover or performance documents
  • ✓You have already complained but received a vague or incomplete reply
