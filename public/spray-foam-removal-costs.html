Removal costs

Removal costs can become the clearest financial loss

Some homeowners are told the spray foam must be removed before a sale, remortgage or survey issue can be resolved. In other cases, the property may need further inspection or remediation before the roof can be properly assessed.

If you were not warned that removal could be difficult or expensive, that may be relevant when reviewing whether the product was properly sold.

The stronger the evidence, the easier it is to understand whether there is a realistic route to challenge the installer, finance provider or responsible party.
