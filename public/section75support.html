You’re Not Stupid — Section 75 Is Often Poorly Explained

Section 75 of the Consumer Credit Act gives important protection when you pay by credit card, but card providers do not always make the process clear or easy.

If goods did not arrive, services were not provided as promised, or what you received was not as described, you may be entitled to a refund even if the retailer is unhelpful, overseas, or no longer trading.
