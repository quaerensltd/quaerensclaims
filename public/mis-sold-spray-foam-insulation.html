Mis-selling review

Spray foam mis-selling is often about what was not explained

A spray foam case may be worth reviewing where the product was sold as safe, approved or risk-free without clear warnings about mortgage, resale, survey or removal issues.

The review should focus on the paperwork, the sales conversation, any finance agreement, the installation documents and what happened later when the property was surveyed or valued.

Quaerens helps organise that evidence so the case can be assessed more clearly.

Common mis-selling warning signs

Mortgage impact not mentioned

The installer did not warn that some lenders may have concerns about spray foam.

Overstated benefits

Claims about savings, protection or property value were too broad or unsupported.

Finance pressure

The product was sold with finance that may not have been clearly explained.
