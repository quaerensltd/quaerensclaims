Why this matters

Parking appeals often turn on practical evidence: where you parked, what the signs said, what you paid, what permit you held and what the operator or council sent afterwards.

What to gather

  • The notice or PCN reference number
  • Photos taken at the location
  • Payment receipts or app screenshots
  • Permit, badge, delivery or loading proof
  • Emails, letters and appeal replies
