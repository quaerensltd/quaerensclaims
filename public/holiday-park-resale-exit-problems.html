Why owners feel trapped

Many owners discover later that resale, transfer, pitch renewal, age limits or park commission make the holiday home difficult to sell or exit without heavy loss.

Documents that help

Purchase documents, park rules, resale terms, commission schedules, valuation records and emails with the park can help show what was disclosed.
