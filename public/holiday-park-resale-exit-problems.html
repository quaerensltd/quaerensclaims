Why owners feel trapped

Many owners discover later that resale, transfer, pitch renewal, age limits or park commission make the holiday home difficult to sell or exit without heavy loss.

Documents that help

Purchase documents, park rules, resale terms, commission schedules, valuation records and emails with the park can help show what was disclosed.

Compensation routes

Reasons why you might be entitled to compensation

Resale restrictions were not explained

Park approval rules, age limits, commission, transfer fees or buyer restrictions can reduce your ability to sell.

The caravan or lodge lost value faster than expected

A sharp gap between the purchase price and realistic resale value may matter where resale promises influenced the sale.

Exit routes were limited or costly

If the park controls removal, resale, pitch transfer or surrender terms, it may be worth reviewing what was disclosed before purchase.
