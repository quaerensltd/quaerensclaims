PCP Car Finance Agreement Concerns?

If your PCP agreement involved unclear commission, unsuitable finance, poor affordability checks or confusing end-of-term terms, it may deserve review.

✅ PCP costs were not clearly explained ✅ Commission or broker incentives were unclear ✅ The finance felt unaffordable ✅ Balloon payment or mileage terms caused problems

Why this matters

PCP agreements can be complex. Disputes often involve how the monthly payment, optional final payment, mileage limits, commission or affordability were explained.

What to gather

Finance agreements, dealer emails, sales documents, affordability information and payment records can help clarify the complaint.
