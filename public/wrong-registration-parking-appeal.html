Why this matters

Small registration mistakes are one of the most common reasons drivers receive parking charges even when they paid. The appeal usually needs to connect the payment record to the vehicle and explain why enforcement is unfair or disproportionate.

What to gather

  • Payment receipt, app record or card statement
  • Vehicle registration and parking location
  • Parking charge notice reference
  • Screenshots from the app or payment portal
  • Photos of payment signs or machine instructions
