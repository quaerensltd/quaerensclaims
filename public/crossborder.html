Legal Framework

  • Regulation (EU) 2015/848 on Insolvency Proceedings – ensures cooperation and recognition across EU states.
  • Regulation (EU) 1215/2012 (Brussels I Recast) – governs recognition and enforcement of civil judgments within the EU.
  • Hague Convention on the Recognition and Enforcement of Foreign Judgments – applicable in certain non-EU cases.
  • UNCITRAL Model Law on Cross-Border Insolvency – adopted in the UK and other key jurisdictions.
  • National Insolvency Laws – applicable based on the debtor’s jurisdiction.
