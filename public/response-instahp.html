The problem

Many owners were not told the full holiday park story.

Many holiday park problems start with promises about rental income, resale value, site fees, finance or lifestyle that later do not match the paperwork or the reality of ownership.

The key issue is often proving what was said, what was signed, what fees were charged, and how the park, finance provider or complaint route responded.

You may need the sales evidence, agreement, fee history and complaint timeline organised before anyone can give the issue a fair review.

Do you qualify?

You may have a review route if any of this has happened.

  • ✓Rental income or resale claims did not match reality
  • ✓Finance, ownership or site rules were not clearly explained
  • ✓Pitch fees, utilities or park charges rose unexpectedly
  • ✓You feel stuck because resale, transfer or exit is difficult
  • ✓The park has delayed, ignored or only partly answered your complaint
