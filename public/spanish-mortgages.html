Who may be eligible?

You can likely claim if:

  • The mortgage was taken out between 2000 and 2019
    Many affected Spanish mortgages fall within this period.
  • Your mortgage included a floor clause or another unfair term
    This includes clauses that limited the benefit of falling rates or imposed unfair costs.
  • The bank did not explain the clause transparently
    Lack of transparency and clear explanation can be a key issue.
  • The mortgage is already settled or the property was sold
    Claims may still be possible in both situations.
