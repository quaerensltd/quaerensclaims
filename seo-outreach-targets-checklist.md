# Backlink and Mention Target Checklist

## Best Starting Targets

- UK consumer advice blogs
- Local community websites with useful links pages
- Money-saving and household budget blogs
- Travel disruption blogs and forums
- Fitness, student and membership advice pages
- Expat and Spain property forums for later Spanish pages
- Homeowner/property blogs for spray foam guidance

## Search Phrases To Find Targets

- "flight delay compensation" "useful links"
- "parking appeal" "resources"
- "private parking charge" "consumer advice"
- "gym cancellation letter" "template"
- "energy switch complaint" "consumer help"
- "train delay claim" "useful links"
- "spray foam mortgage problems" "homeowner"
- "Spanish property problems" "UK buyers"

## Quality Checks Before Contacting

- The page is relevant to the topic.
- The site looks active or recently maintained.
- The page links to other consumer resources.
- The site is not spammy, gambling/adult/crypto-only, or unrelated.
- The contact method is clear.

## Suggested Weekly Workflow

1. Find 10 targets.
2. Add them to the tracker.
3. Send 5 careful emails.
4. Follow up after 7-10 days if no reply.
5. Record every reply or link gained.
6. At month end, compare new referring domains with Search Console impressions.
